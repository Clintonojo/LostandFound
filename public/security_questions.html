Creating Strong Security Questions

Why Security Questions Matter

Security questions help ensure the rightful owner can claim lost items. Choose questions that are easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess.

Guidelines for Strong Questions

  • Avoid using information that can be easily found online.
  • Do not use yes/no questions.
  • Choose answers that won't change over time.
  • Ensure your answers are unique and personal.

Example Items and Suggested Questions

Backpack

Question: "What unusual item do you always carry in this backpack?"

Smartphone

Question: "What is your default ringtone on this device?"

Set of Keys

Question: "What is the color of the keychain attached to this set of keys?"

Jewelry

Question: "What special event did you last wear this jewelry to?"

Umbrella

Question: "What is the unique feature of this umbrella?"

Keep your questions private and do not share your answers with others.
